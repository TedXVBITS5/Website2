Speakers

Syed Bashaar

Syed Bashaar, an electrical engineer turned comic, is known for "Life of a Hyderabadi" & "Life of a Nibba" comedy shows. Founder of "Straight Face Rehab," which aims to mentor Hyderabadi comedians via workshops & open mic contests.

Padmaja Evuri

Padmaja Evuri, a respected happiness and relationship coach, is dedicated to empowering women to thrive in their marriages and lives while prioritising their happiness. With her guidance, individuals navigate relationships with confidence and grace, finding fulfilment and balance.

Heet Tike

Heet Tike, Co-founder of Ripen, is a visionary entrepreneur distinguished for his innovative leadership in transforming brands by leveraging the potential of their customers, employees, partners, and creators to become best sellers in their respective fields.

Dr. Varun Kapoor, IPS
ADGP, Indore

Dr. Varun Kapoor, IPS, Additional Director General of Police, Indore, is a distinguished Indian Police Service officer known for his unwavering dedication to upholding the law and promoting social justice.

Lt. Cdr. Bijay Nair

Bijay Nair, a seasoned former commissioned Naval Officer, made a significant transition into the corporate sector where he now holds the esteemed position of Associate Vice President. His journey reflects a blend of leadership, and determination, shaping him into an inspiring figure.

Lalitha Shedigar Achyutha Nayak

Lalitha Shedigar Achyutha Nayak, VP of Society For Participatory Integrated Development, works tirelessly to prevent sex trafficking and enhance education for children from sex work backgrounds, offering crucial support for a brighter future.

Ramesh Loganathan

Professor Ramesh Loganathan is a prominent figure in the innovation & education sectors, known for his pioneering work with Headstart Network and his role at IIIT Hyderabad. His transition from the corporate world to academia showcases his dedication to fostering innovation & education.

Vidyadhar Kagita

Vidyadhar Kagita, a mechanical engineering graduate turned filmmaker, has directed notable short films like Vara and Vaitarani. His journey showcases audacity and determination in pursuing his passion for filmmaking.
