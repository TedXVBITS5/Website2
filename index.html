What is

TED (Technology, Entertainment, and Design) is a global conference series that serves as a hub for brilliant minds and influential thought leaders from diverse fields. It provides a unique platform for these experts to come together and share their groundbreaking ideas, vast knowledge, and captivating experiences. TED Talks are short, powerful speeches on a wide range of topics that aim to spread ideas and inspire people to take action in their communities. TED has become a popular platform for sharing innovative ideas, and its talks have been viewed by millions of people worldwide.

What is

TEDx events are independently organized events that follow the same format and spirit as TED events. They are organized by local communities and individuals passionate about sharing ideas and bringing people together in their communities. TEDx events feature a combination of live speakers and TED Talk videos, and they cover a wide range of topics that are relevant to their local communities. TEDx events are licensed by TED, which provides guidelines and support to ensure that they meet the standards of quality and integrity that TED is known for.
